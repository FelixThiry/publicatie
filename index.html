Felix Thiry

Graphic designer, videographer
and music producer

https://mobirise.com/

Felix Thiry studied film and photography at the Stedelijke Academie voor Schone Kunsten in Bruges. He graduated in 2014 with his film Irrir Oe Il Sova (The Son of the Owl). 

During his years in Bruges he made  films ranging from comedic to serious but always with a focus to make the viewer transcend as Tarkovsky did.

Altough he doesn't produce as much videos as he used to, it doesn't mean he forgot about his passion for film.His video work can be viewed on YouTube.

Under the alias of Papi Mandarin, Felix Thiry has begun to produce dreamy trap instrumentals as well as spacious and slow ambient works and a chopped and screwed remix of the French cloud rap duo PNL. His remix of Waka Flocka's "Wakapella" can be considerd his most popular track.
